The email address you provide gives consent to receive communications about your precious metals IRA account. You can also elect to receive quarterly statements via email by checking the box at the bottom of the form.

Communications/Electronic Delivery

  • Notices/Updates: By providing your email address, you agree you have access to your email and understand you will receive electronic Communications from Equity Trust Company at no charge. Communications include updated agreements, disclosures, tax forms, invoices and other legally required notices. To receive a paper copy of a Communication, you can contact us.
  • Quarterly Statements: To receive your quarterly statements electronically, please (1) check the box below AND (2) enroll in myEQUITY for your Account. To easily enroll, when you receive your new account number, go to www.trustetc.com, select myEQUITY Login and Register for Online Access. If not enrolled in myEQUITY within 90 days of opening your Account, your statement preference of electronic will be updated to paper delivery and a paper statement Fee will apply (See Fee Schedule).

Should you wish to opt out of certain email communications, such as marketing emails, please go to www.trustetc.com/privacy-policy to opt out of those emails.
